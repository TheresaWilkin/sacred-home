Virtual Trick or Treating!

This year, we'll be taking our trick-or-treating virtual to protect our community during the pandemic.

Each participant will send out twelve small bags of candy, and get the same number of different bags back. On Halloween, we'll do a video call where the kids get to do different activities to get to the different "houses" and open their next little bag. We'll follow this with time to hang out and chat with the other kids and show off their rocking costumes! See the Signup Genius for more details.

All About Us

Sacred Home is a Pagan community in the Greater Philadelphia Area for people who practice Wicca/Paganism/Witchcraft/Eclectic Beliefs/etc. We hold open, family-friendly rituals for the eight holidays in the Wheel of the Year.

We believe that a community is at its best when everyone has a voice, and in that spirit, anyone is welcomed to join the leadership of Sacred Home. Because Black Lives Matter, we are actively seeking Pagans of color to join our leadership.

For more information about Sacred Home and our upcoming events, contact us.

Leadership

Interested in being part of the leadership of Sacred Home? Our leadership is open to anyone who is willing to attend at least 75% of our bimonthly leadership meetings and regularly participate in the organization and/or execution of the rituals and events. All of our meetings and events are being held virtually during the COVID-19 pandemic.

Because Black Lives Matter, we are actively seeking Pagans of color to join our leadership.

All are welcome. Come to a leadership meeting to see what it's all about! Email us at SacredHome.PaganCommunity@gmail.com for information on accessing our meetings.
